FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are Hi-rated deals?

Hi-rated deals are the hot deals in market recommended by majority of the customers or one with high % of savings.

Q: What are Latest deals?

Deals those are posted in recent day(s) or in past few hours, some of these can be part of high rated deals too.

Q: Who can post a deal?

Any customer/moderator/guest that have valid login to ID2D can post or share a deal, it will be reflected in the webpage post approval by moderator. Every contribution is greatly appreciated and valued.

Q: How the deal categorised?

Deals are categorized based on the item details and where they belong to like
Electronics
Health and beauty
Food
Travel
Apparel
Ornaments
Sports
E.t.c
They can be further classified on men/women/kids. Any new category which customer wants to share will be added post moderators approval.

Q: Who can like/dislike a deal?

Any customer who logged in can like a deal or you can use guest login as well, if customer uses his/her registered login points will be accumulated for their contribution, which will not be applicable for guest login.

Q: How many Like/dislike can a user do on one deal?

Per login only one like is allowed, any further likes will be at moderator’s discretion, as likes can be treated as contribution by customer and helps in accumulation of points.

Q: What can be searched in ID2D?

Any deals/coupons shared by customers or posted by moderators can be searched by keywords/key matches in search bar.

Q: How the search works?

Search works/responds similar to any web portal searches by using matching words/keywords or category or by nearest match.

Q: What can be reported as spam?

Any deals/coupons which are not genuine or fake or altered can be reported as spam, it’s the responsibility of all of us to alert every one and save our effort and money.

Q: How the deal/comment can be reported as spam?

Post a comment or alert moderators by going through contact us page or dislike the deal to bring the heat/rating down. It’s our website and we should love to maintain it clean with all your help.

Q: What Actions would be performed on Bad stuff?

Any posts reported as spam and found guilty can be removed from the website and warning will be given for first strike and will be blocked if it is repeated by same person.
